About Us

A team of young, determined, and hardworking individuals who got together to make their dreams come true. The owners have been in in business for over 8 years and they strive to build new businesses to encourage employment in the local areas. The idea of Manchurian was a dream come true. To migrate the delicious recipes of the Hakka style cuisine from the streets of India, seemed easy said than done. However, it was a success. Brought to you by the streets of India (like literally), Hakka style cuisine of the famous Chinese dishes cooked with Indian Spices. Our dishes are cooked in smaller batches and is designed to stay fresh every hour. Cooked to perfection, spicy enough to tickle your taste buds, mouthwatering flavors, and a whole lot of love.
